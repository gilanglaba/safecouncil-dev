Research UN publications, academic papers, and industry reports relevant to SafeCouncil's positioning and development.

## Instructions

When the user invokes `/research`, ask what topic or angle they want to explore if not specified. Common research areas:

- UN/UNICC AI governance policies and frameworks
- Academic papers on multi-agent AI evaluation or LLM-as-judge approaches
- Responsible AI deployment in international organizations
- AI risk assessment methodologies
- Comparative studies of AI safety evaluation tools

## Steps

1. **Search the web** using WebSearch for relevant publications, papers, and reports.

2. **Fetch and read** key sources using WebFetch when deeper analysis is needed.

3. **Summarize findings** through three lenses:
   - **Academic paper angle**: What research gaps does this address? How does it support SafeCouncil's methodology?
   - **UN whitepaper angle**: What policy implications? How does this align with UN AI governance goals?
   - **Startup angle**: What market insights? How does this inform SafeCouncil's differentiation?

4. **Cite sources** with titles, authors, dates, and URLs.

5. **Recommend next steps**: What should SafeCouncil build or adapt based on the findings?
